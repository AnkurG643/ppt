The Role of Entrepreneurship in Economic Development

Date: August 5, 2025
1

Introduction to Entrepreneurship

What is Entrepreneurship?

  • The process of creating, launching, and managing new ventures
  • Involves identifying opportunities, taking calculated risks
  • Combines innovation with business acumen

Global Entrepreneurship Statistics:

  • 665 million entrepreneurs worldwide (2024)
  • 1 in 8 working-age people engaged in entrepreneurial activity
  • Global startup ecosystem growth: 21% annually
  • India: 3rd largest startup ecosystem globally
2

Job Creation & Employment Impact

Direct Job Creation:

  • Indian startups: 16.6 lakh direct jobs (2016-2024)
  • Small businesses employ 60-70% of workforce globally
  • Young firms create 50% of new jobs (OECD average)

Indirect Impact:

  • Multiplier effect: 1 startup job creates 2-3 additional jobs
  • Supply chain employment
  • Service sector growth

Sector-wise Employment (India, Oct 2024):

  • IT Services: 2.04 lakh jobs
  • Healthcare: 1.47 lakh jobs
  • Professional Services: 94,000 jobs
  • Education: 90,414 jobs

Competitive Benefits:

Poverty Reduction & Social Development

Income Generation:

Regional Development:

  • 51% of Indian startups from Tier-2/3 cities

Social Impact:
